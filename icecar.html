🎯 Verdict for your trip (TUE–FRI, 4 days)

Best overall:Jeep Wrangler Rubicon @ Go Car Rental — handles F225/F208 to Landmannalaugar with confidence, looks amazing in Iceland photos, total cost ~1 640–2 090 SEK per person including fuel. The sweet spot between cool factor, capability, and price.
Best value:Dacia Duster @ Lotus with Platinum insurance (code LOTUSD5). Proven on your exact route, river crossing covered. Total ~680–830 SEK per person. You just won't love the car itself.
Dark horse pick:Toyota Hilux @ Lotus — same price bracket as Wrangler but more rugged/workhorse than sexy. Great for Landmannalaugar specifically. Pickup bed is actually useful for wet/muddy gear after hikes.
Premium splurge:Land Rover Defender @ Blue Car — if you want the coolest car that's also the most comfortable. ~2 100–2 800 SEK per person. Big jump in price but zero compromises.
Insurance note:For Landmannalaugar via F225 you need gravel + F-road coverage. Lotus Platinum is unique in Iceland for covering river crossing engine damage. If going Wrangler or Defender via Go Car or Blue Car, explicitly confirm F-road and river coverage before booking.
2026 road tax:Iceland introduced a km-based road tax in 2026 (~7–8 ISK/km). At 850 km that's ~6 000–7 000 ISK (~55–65 €) extra — some providers include it in the price, others bill separately. Ask when booking.

All prices are estimates based on publicly available 2026 data. 4-day September shoulder-season rates will be lower than July–August peak. Actual prices vary by availability and insurance package chosen. Currency conversion used: 1€ ≈ 11 SEK.
