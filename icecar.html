🎯 Final recommendation

Best overall:Jeep Wrangler + Gold insurance @ Go Car — $1,125 all-in for 3 days, $281/person. Gravel covered, zero excess, F-roads confirmed, looks great. Book directly at gocarrental.is.
Best value surprise:Volvo XC90 @ Saga (Northbound) — only SEK 6,806 (~$654) with CDW/TPL/TP already included. Cheapest premium live price found. Good for comfort-first if F-road capability isn't top priority.
Gold vs Platinum:Gold is the sweet spot for your trip. Platinum adds sand/ash + tire protection which are nice-to-haves in Iceland, but the $52 difference is worth it for peace of mind given Sep weather and Landmannalaugar volcanic terrain.
⚠️ Note:Go Car Silver includes $1,153 excess liability and $288 gravel excess — do NOT go with Silver on F-roads. Gold minimum.
